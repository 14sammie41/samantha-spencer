Who am I?

As a passionate customer services and sales worker, I am pleased to be able to apply for this position, to which I hope to bring experience and enthusiasm. Over the years I have been involved in many community projects both locally and abroad. I pride myself on being able to engage a team of people to work as one. My time in retail has taught me about managing people at all levels. As a full time manager I demonstrated considerable flexibility, prioritizing the business at all times. As a member of this team I learnt how to cope in a high pressure retail atmosphere. Fundraising taught me the importance of giving back to both the community and the world in as many ways as I can. My ambition to progress in everything I do means that I set myself high standards. I am very confident when working with people and have shown my ability to teach and train on many occasions. The variety of things I have done and situations I have been in not only in retail but also in restaurant, Fundraising and office work means I have a very broad outlook on any career I’m in. I’m very happy and willing to learn to gain new skills and reach the best of my ability in everything I do.
