What have I done?

In my younger years, I did (like most) a fair bit of bouncing between jobs. Starting in a local restaurant gave me a passion for customer service, finding out that good service made people happy (and pay tips) was such a big motivator. Naturally i grew out of my local restaurant and wanted to try something new, so i started looking around and found fundraising. It felt logical to move into something which gave me the ability to give back whilst talking to people. I grew out of this as the life style was pretty hectic and i was looking to slow down a bit. From there I came back home and back to what i knew, hospitality. Working in a bar was excellent fun and allowed me to setlle back in to a more stable home life. When looking for a more stable income i went down the road and found our local high street convenience store was looking. That was the beginning of eight years of retail obsession. When I broke my foot in 2024 I had to take some time out of retail to heal and re found my love for computers. This lead me to finding my first work from home job and then to taking on a Diploma in Web Application Development.

My career so far...

Brewers Fayre & The Poacher

Working in restaurants and bars was excellent fun for me, as a natural talker serving was easy for me. I had the opportunity to work in every area of the restaurant and took the opportunity to assist in the day to day running of the bar too.

McColl's, Sainsbury's & Argos

As a Supervisor at McColls I was responsible for most of the day to day running of the shop in the absense of management. I was also responsible for stock management and working deliveries.

Fundraising & Work From Home

Door to Door fundraising for Home Fundraising was an amazing opportunity to work for some of the UK's biggest charities. More recently, due to an injury, i took the opportunity to work from home for HGS as part of the Warm Home Discount support team.

Level 5 Diploma

Alongside the career changes made as a result of my injury in 2024 I re found my love of computers. So i took the leap and now i'm learning how to code through Code Institute.
